　About the product
  • About delivered products:
    We check the stock at any time, but you may have to wait depending on the stock status. In the unlikely event that it is out of stock, we may cancel your order. In that case, we will contact you by email or phone.
  • Consumption tax:
    Consumption tax (10%) is included in the selling price of the products listed in the online shop. * A consumption tax rate of 8% is applied to products that support the reduced tax rate.
  • Product Warranty: The
    product warranty card shipped from the Fuji Medical Instrument Online Shop is not stamped due to reasons such as the delivery method of our shop.
    By attaching the "Product Shipment Notification Email" (shipping completion email) to the warranty card, which will be the proof of purchase, it will be the proof of the purchase at our shop and the date of purchase. (* The purchase date will be the date when the "Product Shipment Notification Email" is sent.) Please keep the "Product Shipment Notification Email" in a safe place.
    The warranty period of the product is based on the warranty attached to the purchased product. After the warranty period expires, you will be charged. ( Manufacturer's warranty )
    There is no manufacturer's warranty for some products. We will respond for a fee. Please see here
    for the extended warranty .

    The sale and purchase contract for the goods shall be closed when the customer presses the button to agree to the order.
    However, if the product cannot be prepared due to unavoidable circumstances, or if the customer's payment is not completed, we may cancel the order. (We will contact you)
  • Cancellation after ordering:
    In principle, we will not accept cancellation due to customer's convenience after payment or shipping of the product.
