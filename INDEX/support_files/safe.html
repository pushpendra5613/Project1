Request for proper use of household electric massager
[Released on February 1, 2010]

The household electric massager is designed on the assumption that it will be used correctly according to the instruction manual. Never intentionally remove the cover (cloth, leather, etc.) of a household electric massager or use it in a torn state, as it is extremely dangerous. The Japan Home Health Equipment Association has also issued the following documents to call attention to this matter.

"Request for proper use of home massager" Notice poster

By using the massager with the cloth cover cut off, a fatal accident has occurred in which clothes are caught in the internal drive unit.

【caveat】

● Never remove or cut off the attached cloth cover as it is extremely dangerous.

● If the cloth cover is torn or punctured due to wear, etc., stop using it immediately.

● Never repair or modify by yourself.

Notices and requests about the "massage machine" that has been used for many years
[Released on December 2, 2016]

From the viewpoint of safety, please discontinue use of old massage machines that have been used for many years and have fir balls.
Massage machines that have been used for many years may emit smoke or ignite due to aging deterioration of electrical parts such as motors, cords, and capacitors.
In addition, even if the product has been stored for many years without being used, the parts may deteriorate.

If you experience any of the following symptoms, stop using it immediately and unplug the power plug from the outlet.

● It smells dark

● Slow movement, sometimes immobility, weakening of massage power

● There is an abnormal sound

● The power cord and plug are abnormally hot

● When I move the power cord, it moves or stops working.

● The power plug has been plugged in for many years, and dust and moisture have accumulated.

For inquiries and questions, please contact the "Customer Service Desk" below.
