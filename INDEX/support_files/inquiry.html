Inquiries from the homepage 

If you would like to make inquiries from our website, please agree to our privacy policy and fill out the form below.

● We will reply to you as soon as possible except Saturdays, Sundays, public holidays, year-end and New Year holidays, and our holidays. It may take some time to reply. In addition, we may not be able to reply to you depending on the content. Thank you as you acknowledge it beforehand.

● Depending on the content of your inquiry, we may reply to you by phone, fax, or letter instead of email. Please note.

● Our reply will be sent to the individual customer. Reprinting part or all of it, secondary use, or use for other purposes is strictly prohibited.

● In rare cases, we may not be able to reply to you due to a wrong e-mail address or system failure.

●弊社のシステムの都合上、フリーメールからのお問い合わせは、受付ができない場合がございます。

●お問い合わせフォームで入力された情報は、SSLによって暗号化され、お客様の個人情報を保護しています。また、お問い合わせフォームにご記入いただいた個人情報は、当社のプライバシーポリシーに基づき、適切にお取り扱いいたします。詳しくはプライバシーポリシーをご覧ください。

●報道関係の方からの取取材依頼・プレスリリースに関するお問い合わせはこちらからお願いいたします。
