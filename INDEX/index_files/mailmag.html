\ Introduction of e-mail magazine member benefits! /


Benefit 1 We will inform you about new product information etc. sent from Fuji Medical Instrument.


Benefit 2 We will inform e-mail magazine members of advantageous sale information as soon as possible.


Benefit 3 We will deliver a column about seasons and health.


OurPlease check the privacy policy and the terms of use of the e-mail newsletter, and register as an e-mail newsletter member.

How to register


(1) Enter your e-mail address, name, gender, date of birth, and prefecture of residence, and then click the "Confirm" button.

② A confirmation screen will be displayed. Please click the "Send" button if you like.

③ The registration completion screen will be displayed, and an automatic reply email will be sent to the registered email address. This completes the registration procedure.

* Notes on reception settings
If you have specified the domain in the email settings of your smartphone or mobile phone, please set so that you can receive emails from "@ fujiiryoki.jp".
If you use the junk mail filter, the auto-reply mail may be sorted to the "Junk E-mail Folder". Change the junk e-mail filter settings.


Change registration information


① Please enter your currently registered email address and name. After entering, click the "Confirm" button.

(2) Information on your currently registered name, email address, prefecture, gender, and date of birth will be displayed. Enter the items you need to change and click the "Send" button.

③ A confirmation screen will be displayed. Please click the "Send" button if you like.

③ The change completion screen will be displayed, and an automatic reply email will be sent to the registered email address. This completes the change procedure.

Click here to change your registration information


Undelivered (stopped) information


① Please enter the currently registered email address. After entering, click the "Confirm" button.

② A confirmation screen will be displayed. Please click the "Send" button if you like.

③ The cancellation completion screen will be displayed, and an automatic reply email will be sent to the registered email address. This completes the procedure for canceling (suspending) delivery.
