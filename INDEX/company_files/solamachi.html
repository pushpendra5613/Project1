Tokyo Solamachi store

Information on Tokyo Solamachi store


Our store on the 5th floor of the Tokyo Solamachi East Yard offers a relaxing space where you can see and experience the “evolution of massage chairs”.

In the space where you can experience the massage chair for a fee (200 yen for 15 minutes), we have prepared usage guides in multiple languages ​​(Japanese, English, Chinese, Korean), so foreign customers can try it with confidence. You can.
In addition, the history of massage chairs and the transition of technology are explained in Japanese and English panels.

Efforts at the Solamachi store have been certified under the "beyond2020 program". ( * )

  • business hours

    10: 00-21: 00

    * Same as Tokyo Solamachi business hours.

  • place

    Tokyo Solamachi East Yard 5th floor

  • location

    1-1-2 Oshiage, Sumida-ku, Tokyo 131-0045 Tokyo
    Skytree Town Solamachi East Yard 5th floor

  • access

    * By train
    Immediately from the front exit of Tobu Skytree Line "Tokyo Skytree Station"
    Tobu Skytree Line / Hanzomon Line / Keisei Line / Toei Asakusa Line "Oshiage Station" B3F Exit / A2 Exit

    * If you come by car, please use the pay parking lot.


What is the beyond2020 program?

Looking ahead to 2020 and beyond, the Cabinet Secretariat Tokyo Olympic Games is a cultural program that contributes to the creation of a legacy that is suitable for a mature society and can be proud of to the next generation by utilizing Japan's strengths of regional and diverse culture. The secretariat of the Games and Tokyo Paralympic Games Promotion Headquarters certifies and attaches the logo mark, so that we can expand to all over Japan with a sense of unity.
