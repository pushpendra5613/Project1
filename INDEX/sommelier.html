Sommelier course video & sommelier call 

At Fuji Medical Instruments, we offer support services "Sommelier Course Video" and "Sommelier Call" that guide the massage chair sommelier how to use the massage chair.

  • Information on sommelier call

Sommelier course video 2021/9/16 update

This is a service where the massage chair sommelier introduces how to use the massage chair, such as how to sit properly and the recommended automatic course. You can view it as many times as you like from your smartphone or computer. It is being updated on the " Fuji Medical Instrument Official YouTube Channel ".

Information on sommelier call 

At Sommelier Ecole, you can contact us by phone for consultation on how to use the massage chair (* target model). A sommelier advisor who has completed the training by the massage chair sommelier will guide you on how to use it, so please feel free to contact us with any questions.

The advisor will also respond according to the customer's operation while actually sitting on the massage chair.
