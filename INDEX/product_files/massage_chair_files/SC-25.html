Equipped with "Deep Extreme Mechanical PRO".
Feels like a technique

Massage chair SC-25

Released on November 16, 2021
Medical device approval number: 229ABBZX00091A01
This product is for specific companies.

  • Product Top
  • Features
  • Remote control /
    basic functions
  • specification

Main features

Feature 1Unique function

"Deep Pole Mecha Pro" that loosens the stiffness deeper and more delicately

Achieves a maximum amount of protrusion of a fir ball of about 12.5 cm that approaches the deep layers of muscles. Two fir balls are driven up and down, left and right, and back and forth, and even that time is precisely controlled. You can experience the feeling of rubbing like a technique.


More details

Feature 2

"Air massage" that wraps the whole body and gently heals

Gently wrap the whole body with 31 air bags and massage. Relieves fatigue comfortably. Equipped with a strength adjustment function for each part.

More details

Feature 3

Since the soles of the feet are easy to get tired, carefully "grab the soles of the feet and shiatsu"

By combining the ankle and instep air bags and the rollers on the sole of the foot, massage to grab the foot.

More details

Feature 4

22 types of automatic courses including 5 long-term courses "30-minute specialized course"

It is equipped with 22 types of automatic courses, including a "30-minute specialized course" that allows you to take time to heal your daily fatigue.


More details

Feature 5Unique function

"Double heater" to be healed by gentle warmth

Equipped with an instant heat multi-heater and sole heater function built into the back pad. It warms the back, tummy, and toes to support massage.


More details

Purpose or effect

Anma, a substitute for massage. For general household use.

  • Fatigue recovery
  • Improve blood circulation
  • Get rid of muscle fatigue
  • Loosen muscle stiffness
  • Relief of neuralgia and muscle pain

SC-25 is a target model of "Sommelier Ecole".

  • ・ In "Sommelier Call", the sommelier advisor will accept questions and inquiries about how to use the sommelier by phone and will guide you. ※free
