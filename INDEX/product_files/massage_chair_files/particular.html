Discerning massage chair

There is a reason why Fuji Medical Instrument's massage chairs continue to be loved.

POINT1

Reliable track record as a pioneer of massage chairs

Since developing the first machine in 1954, Fuji Medical Instruments has developed and sold various massage chairs to heal people's fatigue.

  • The first "Fuji automatic massage machine" certified as a mechanical engineering heritage

  • "FM-52" which adopted a special clutch that can be rubbed and beaten with 2 motors

  • AS-003 was a big hit with the 3D point navigation system installed.

More details

POINT2

Various functions realized by industry-leading advanced technology

The "5D-AI mechanism" that automatically adjusts the force adjustment, movement speed, and pressure in real time according to the information obtained from the load condition of the user's body, and high-precision sensing technology automatically detect the shoulder height and spine line. However, we have developed a series of original new functions such as "AI Double Sensing +" that detects appropriate massage points regardless of body shape and height. It is evaluated as.

  • "5D-AI Mecha" that realizes a smooth AI massage like a procedure

  • "AI Double Sensing +" that automatically detects appropriate massage points regardless of body shape or height

  • "High-performance air system" that can adjust the strength in 7 steps

More details

POINT3

"Massage Chair Meister" to get closer to human massage

At Fuji Medical Instruments, there are employees who play the role of "Massage Chair Meister" in order to get as close as possible to the massage performed by humans. The adjustment work by the massage chair Meister to pursue the "comfort" unique to humans using their own body in 0.1 second increments is performed until the final stage of development, realizing a "machine-like" massage. I am.

  • Adjustment work by the massage chair Meister. I'm not just resting.

  • Detailed settings in 0.1 second increments will lead to "comfort" for more people.

  • "Meister Team" where staff of various body shapes gather

More details

POINT4

"Massage chair sommelier" that conveys the charm of massage chairs

Fuji Medical Instruments has an employee called "Massage Chair Sommelier" who is a specialist who conveys the charm of massage chairs.
We are familiar with various massage chairs in detail and give advice that suits our customers' concerns.

  • A sommelier that conveys the charm of a massage chair. I am working every day to inject the energy of tomorrow's Japan.

  • A sommelier that explains the functions while letting you experience it. I can understand the reason with this comfort, so I nod a lot!

  • A sommelier who conveys his know-how at the workshop. The day when the disciple will play an active role as a sommelier is near.
